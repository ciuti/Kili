About

We are The University of Nairobi Scouts

Kili rovers comprises of students from all the University of Nairobi campuses which include; Chiromo, Kikuyu, Main, Parklands, Upper Kabete, Lower Kabete, ADD and Kenya Science .

We are under Westlands Subcounty Scouts

Kili Rovers are registered as a rover crew under Westlands sub-county. Kili leadership is made up of a council of rovers elected by Kili rovers under the guidance of our scout patron
